../GliderWinchItems/BMS/hw/eagle/Adapt-BQto1818/A18-BQ

This wires the ribbon/IDC for the 18 cell battery module
layout to the 16 cell keyed header on the bmsbmsBQ pcb.

A female 2x10 header (non-keyed) mounts on the left 
side on the bottom and mates with the BQ pcb.

A male 2x10 keyed header mounts on the top and
receives the ribbon IDC connector.
